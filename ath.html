Athletics

PSHS-CRC offers you the opportunity to showcase and refine not only your academic prowess but also your abilities and skills in extracurricular activities like sports and athletics. Scholars have the opportunity to showcase and compete their talents and athletic skills against students from both upper and lower batches at annual activities like the Intramurals. Through their participation in extracurricular activities like the Indoor and Outdoor Sports Club, they also refine their skills.
