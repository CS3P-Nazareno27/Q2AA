Philippine Science High School (PSHS) is a prestigious high school fit for those who excel in Sciences and Mathematics. Being the thirteenth campus of this school in the entire Philippines, Philippine Science High School, Caraga- Region-Campus (PSHS-CRC), encompasses almost 600 students, with 120 students for the current batches 2028, and 2029, and 90 students for the upper batches. As the years progress, the number of students for each batch will be approximately 120. Situated in Butuan City's Barangay Ampayon, the school is renowned for its expansive grounds and superbly designed buildings and infrastructure. Constructed with white walls and windows tinged with blue, the campus has a distinctive architectural style that stands out from the rest. The outstanding academics of PSHS-CRC are another thing to be proud of, as they represent the school well in national and international competitions, winning numerous honors for the campus. This school aims to develop these high school students into future leaders and scientists of the nation.
