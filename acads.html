Learn about the Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus's academic structure and subjects.

ACADEMIC GOALS

The academic structure and goal of our institution is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). The curriculum is rigorous and focuses on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and research skills.

SUBJECT OFFERINGS

Our school offers a wide range of subjects that cover various STEM disciplines. Students engage in in-depth studies in subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science, Researches, and many more. The curriculum is designed to inspire students to excel in their chosen stem fields.

RESEARCH OPPORTUNITIES

The PSHS-Institution encourages students to participate in research projects. Our school provides opportunities for students to collaborate with teachers and engage in hands-on research experiences, fostering a culture of curiosity, critical analysis, and innovation.
